About Us

Welcome to our first-semester project website! This project is designed to showcase the comparison of different iPhone models, from iPhone 11 to iPhone 16. Our goal is to help users easily understand the features, specifications, and improvements in each model.

We are a group of enthusiastic students learning web development and AI basics. Through this project, we aim to improve our skills in HTML, CSS, and responsive web design, while creating an interactive and informative website for users.

This website is a demonstration of our understanding of web design principles, structured content, and user-friendly layout. We hope it provides a clear and engaging experience for anyone looking to compare iPhone models.

Our Objectives:

  • Provide clear and accurate comparisons of iPhone models to help users make informed decisions.
  • Demonstrate basic web development skills learned during the first semester.
  • Create a visually appealing and responsive website that is easy to navigate.
  • Practice organizing content logically and presenting it in a professional format.

Future Goals:

  • Enhance the website with interactive features such as filters, search, and user ratings.
  • Include comparisons for other brands and devices in addition to iPhones.
  • Implement backend functionality to store user reviews and feedback securely.
  • Continue learning and applying advanced web development techniques to improve usability and design.

We believe this project reflects our commitment to learning and creating useful, educational content. Thank you for visiting our website, and we hope you find it helpful and informative!
